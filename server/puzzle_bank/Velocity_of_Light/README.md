# Velocity of Light

**Objective**: Calculate the intercept time for the radiation front.

A massive solar flare has erupted. We need to know exactly when the radiation front will reach our deep-space monitoring satellite stationed at the **L1 Lagrange point**.

**Data**:
- Distance from Sun to L1: **148,500,000 km**.
- Speed of Light ($c$): **300,000 km/s**.

**The Task**:
Calculate the time in **seconds** ($t = \frac{d}{c}$) it takes for the first rays of the flare to reach the satellite.

Round your answer to the nearest whole second.
